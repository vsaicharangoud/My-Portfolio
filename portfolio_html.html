Who Am I?

I'm a B.E. Electronics & Communication Engineering student at Vasavi College of Engineering, Hyderabad (CGPA: 8.45), with a strong foundation built through a Diploma at Jawaharlal Nehru Government Polytechnic (CGPA: 9.47).
